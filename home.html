    City of River
    Chandpur is famously known as the city of rivers, where the Padma, Meghna, and Dakatia meet. The mesmerizing river views attract thousands of visitors every year. It is also popular for its delicious Hilsa fish, considered the pride of Chandpur. People from across Bangladesh visit to enjoy fresh Hilsa from the rivers. The peaceful environment, natural beauty, and boat rides make Chandpur a perfect place for travelers seeking both adventure and relaxation.

    The journey of Padma River

    The Padma River flows gracefully through Chandpur, carrying stories of life and nature along its banks. During the evening, the river glows with the colors of the sunset, creating a magical view that mesmerizes every visitor. Locals often gather by the riverside to enjoy the fresh breeze and watch fishing boats return with their daily catch.

    Chandpur is a unique destination for river tourism in Bangladesh. The city offers scenic boat rides where travelers can witness the meeting point of the Padma and Meghna rivers, locally called “Mohona.” This natural beauty, combined with fresh river fish markets and traditional river life, gives Chandpur its charm. For those who seek to experience the real essence of Bangladesh, Chandpur is truly an unforgettable place.
